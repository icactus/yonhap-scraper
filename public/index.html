Kim Il-sung University Considers Establishing Representative Office at Siberian Science Base

4/2/2025 Original Article
Novosibirsk State University (NSU) announced through its homepage newsletter on the 2nd that 'Novosibirsk State University, the Standing Committee of the Siberian Branch of the Russian Academy of Sciences, and the Novosibirsk Akademgorodok Science and Technology Park are actively working to find a location for the Kim Il-sung University representative office'
This article reports on Kim Il-sung University's potential establishment of a representative office in Akademgorodok, a science base in Siberia, Russia. The initiative aims to strengthen scientific and educational cooperation between North Korea and Russia, fostering joint research and exchange programs. This development signifies a notable effort to cultivate international collaborations for academic advancement, possibly to circumvent sanctions and/or gain access to science and technology.
