North Korea's Mount Baekdu Listed as UNESCO Global Geopark

4/11/2025 Original Article
The Baektu Mountain is characterized by its magnificent landscape formed by volcanic eruptions
The North Korean part of Mount Baekdu has been designated as a UNESCO Global Geopark, following the designation of the Chinese part last year, making the entire mountain a Global Geopark. This is North Korea's first such designation, recognizing the area's geological value and commitment to sustainable management. The area is notable for its volcanic landscape and glacial landforms, with potential for future eruptions, having previously registered with UNESCO in 1989 as a World Biosphere Reserve.

UN: 67 Maternal Deaths per 100,000 Live Births in North Korea... 17 Times Higher than South Korea

4/10/2025 Original Article
North Korea's maternal mortality ratio was estimated at 67 per 100,000 live births in 2023
A recent UN report indicates that maternal mortality rates in North Korea have decreased significantly since the 'Arduous March' but remain 17 times higher than in South Korea. In 2023, North Korea's maternal mortality ratio was estimated at 67 deaths per 100,000 live births, compared to 4 in South Korea. While progress has been made in the last two decades, North Korea still falls short of the global target of reducing maternal mortality to below 70 per 100,000 live births by 2030.
